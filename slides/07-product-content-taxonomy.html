Advanced Taxonomy & Product Data Model

Owning vs. non-owning categories, rule-based classification, and deep hierarchies

📂

Owning (Physical) Taxonomy

  • Single primary category per product
  • Deep nested hierarchies supported
  • Referential integrity enforced
  • Product inherits category-level attributes
  • PDP breadcrumb always from primary leaf node
  • No product duplication across categories
🔭

Non-Owning (Rule-Based) Categories

  • Business users configure via UI (no code)
  • Rules use: attributes, facets, conditional logic
  • Product appears in multiple trees simultaneously
  • Independent from owning classification
  • Scheduled rule execution & materialization
  • Export resolved paths (UI + Excel)
